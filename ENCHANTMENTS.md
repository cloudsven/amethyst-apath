### Rupture (Levels 1-4)
An enchantment found in the Trial Chambers, used on the [Scarlet Mace](ITEMS.md#scarlet-mace).
It can be used on normal maces however the enchantment does not do anything.

Each level increases the percentage of armor durability damage by 2.5%. 
With Rupture 4 damaging armor durability by 15% of it's max durability.
