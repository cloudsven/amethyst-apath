### ![amirite shard](/assets/amirite_shard.png "amirite shard") Amirite Shard
The main ingredient used to craft Amirite tools & weapons.

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Netherite Ingot
- 4 Amethyst Clusters
- 4 Quartz

![amirite shard recipe](/assets/amirite_shard_recipe.png "amirite shard recipe")

### ![amirite totem](/assets/amirite_totem.png "amirite totem") Amirite Totem
Like a normal totem, but when used it explodes and deals 2.5 hearts of true damage to any nearby player/mob (ignores armor).

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Totem of Undying
- 4 Amirite Shards

![amirite totem recipe](/assets/amirite_totem_recipe.png "amirite totem recipe")

### Amirite Shield
Reduces damage taken by 25% when holding it and reflects 10% of damage when blocking.

**Abilities:**
- [KNOCK 'EM] On right click, all nearby players/mobs are knocked back and 1 heart of true damage is dealt to them.
  (10s cooldown)

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Shield
- 4 Amirite Shards

![amirite shield recipe](/assets/amirite_shield_recipe.png "amirite shield recipe")

### Amirite Crossbow
Like a normal crossbow, but the arrow explodes and deals 2 hearts of true damage to the target.

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Crossbow
- 4 Amirite Shards

![amirite crossbow recipe](/assets/amirite_crossbow_recipe.png "amirite crossbow recipe")

### ![amirite cleaver](/assets/amirite_cleaver.png "amirite cleaver") Amirite Cleaver
Like a netherite axe, but deals 8 hearts of damage with an additional 1 heart of true damage. However the attack speed is very slow.
It will also drop the head of whatever mob you killed. (if it has a head block)

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Blaze Rod
- 4 Amirite Shards

![amirite cleaver recipe](/assets/amirite_cleaver_recipe.png "amirite cleaver recipe")

### ![scarlet heart](/assets/scarlet_heart.png "scarlet heart") Scarlet Heart
Found in a structure called the [Amethyst Chasm](STRUCTURES.md#amethyst-chasm).
It is used to craft the [Charged Scarlet Heart](ITEMS.md#-charged-scarlet-heart).

### ![charged scarlet heart](/assets/scarlet_heart_charged.png "charged scarlet heart") Charged Scarlet Heart
The main ingredient used to craft Scarlet weapons & tools.

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Scarlet Heart
- 4 Amirite Shards
- 4 Nether Stars
  
![charged scarlet heart recipe](/assets/scarlet_heart_charged_recipe.png "charged scarlet heart recipe")

### Scarlet Blade
Like a normal Netherite Sword, but it damages the targets armor by 3% of it's max durability per hit. However, it also damages the users armor by 5% of it's max durability per hit.

**Abilities:**
- [SCARLET DRAIN] On swing, the target gets Wither II for 3 seconds.
- [SCARLET SNATCH] On swing, the user is healed by 10% of the damage dealt.
- [CRITICAL FIRE] On crit, the sword deals 8 seconds of fire to the target.
- [GLORY KILL] On the target's death, the user is healed by how much health the target had before dying.

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Netherite Sword (enchants get transferred)
- 1 Charged Scarlet Heart
- 1 Blaze Rod
- 2 Amirite Shards
- 2 Netherite Ingots

![scarlet blade recipe](/assets/scarlet_blade_recipe.png "scarlet blade recipe")

### Scarlet Pickaxe
Can break unbreakable blocks after 5 seconds, however it does not drop them.

LIST OF UNBREAKABLE BLOCKS:
- Bedrock
- End Portal Frame
- Reinforced Deepslate
- Trial Spawner
- Trial Vault

**Abilities:**
- [SCARLET HASTE] On right click, the user is granted Haste II for 1 minute. (5m cooldown)

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Netherite Pickaxe (enchants get transferred)
- 1 Charged Scarlet Heart
- 1 Blaze Rod
- 2 Amirite Shards
- 2 Netherite Ingots

![scarlet pickaxe recipe](/assets/scarlet_pickaxe_recipe.png "scarlet pickaxe recipe")

### Scarlet Mace
Like a normal mace, however it damages the targets armor by 5% of it's max durability.
And that percentage can be increased with an enchant called [Rupture.](ENCHANTMENTS.md#rupture-levels-1-4)
However, the durability runs out very fast.

**Abilities:**
- [SCARLET DRAIN] On swing, the target gets Wither II for 3 seconds.
- [SCARLET LAUNCH] On right click, the user is launched into the sky. (20s cooldown)

Crafting recipe:
- 1 Mace (enchants get transferred)
- 1 Charged Scarlet Heart
- 1 Blaze Rod
- 2 Amirite Shards
- 2 Netherite Ingots

![scarlet mace recipe](/assets/scarlet_mace_recipe.png "scarlet mace recipe")
